About Us

We are a group of students who are advertising our delivery system based on drones.

You can get your packages delivered right to your doorstep without needing the postman coming straight up to your door and having to sign for it but rather have a drone which drops the package outside of your door, you pick it up and release the drone.

And there you go! Package delivered!
